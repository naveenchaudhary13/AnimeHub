About AnimeHub

Made with ❤️ by Naveen Chaudhary

AnimeHub is your one-stop destination for high-quality anime streaming. Discover, watch, and enjoy the latest and most popular anime series and movies – all in one place.

Built with Django and Tailwind CSS, AnimeHub offers a seamless experience with a sleek UI and fast loading times. Whether you're a casual viewer or a hardcore otaku, there's something here for everyone.

Why AnimeHub?

  • ✔️ Clean, minimal, and fast UI
  • ✔️ Organized by genres and episodes
  • ✔️ Built for anime lovers, by anime lovers
  • ✔️ 100% Free and Open Source
